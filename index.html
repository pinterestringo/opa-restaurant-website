Our Story

Where the Streets of Greece Meet Shkodër

OPA Greek Street was born from a love of Greek culture and street food — the kind you find at a busy corner in Athens or Thessaloniki, wrapped in warm pita and bursting with flavour.

Every gyro is slow-roasted, every tzatziki whipped fresh, and every pita baked to order. We bring the spirit of the Greek street corner straight to Shkodër.

We believe fast food can be real food — so we never cut corners on ingredients, only on waiting time.
